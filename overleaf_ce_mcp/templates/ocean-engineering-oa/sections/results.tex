\section{Results}

\subsection{Experimental setup}
The evaluation combines public and engineering datasets. Metrics include error-based indicators and engineering utility indicators. Baselines cover both classical and recent representative methods.

\subsection{Main results}
The main results are summarized in tables and figures. Compared with the strongest baseline, the proposed method provides consistent gains on primary metrics with acceptable computational overhead.

\subsection{Ablation study}
Ablation studies show that each key module contributes positively to final performance, and the constraint component provides the largest robustness gain under extreme operating conditions.
