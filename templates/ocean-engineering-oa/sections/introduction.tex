\section{Introduction}

Ocean engineering systems often operate in highly uncertain environments where design and operation must balance safety, economy, and sustainability. Existing methods still suffer from limited generalization, reduced accuracy under complex conditions, or high computational cost.

To address these challenges, this paper proposes a data-driven method tailored for ocean engineering scenarios and validates it on benchmark and scenario-specific datasets. The main contributions are as follows:
\begin{itemize}
  \item A reproducible workflow for problem formulation and solution.
  \item Key modules for improved robustness in marine conditions.
  \item Systematic experiments demonstrating improvements on critical metrics.
\end{itemize}
