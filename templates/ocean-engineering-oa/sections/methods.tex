\section{Methods}

\subsection{Problem formulation}
Let $\mathbf{x}$ denote the input variables and $y$ the target output. The objective is to learn a mapping $f:\mathbf{x}\mapsto y$ under practical constraints while minimizing prediction error and engineering cost.

\subsection{Model architecture}
The method consists of data preprocessing, a core solver, and post-processing modules. The core solver adopts a multi-stage optimization strategy and constraint terms to improve convergence stability and physical plausibility.

\subsection{Training and implementation details}
Training uses stratified sampling. Optimizer settings and hyper-parameters are provided in supplementary materials. Experiments are repeated under a unified hardware environment, and mean/variance statistics are reported.
